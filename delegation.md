# Delegation

#### Delegate power to amplify and earn from other curators.

* Delegate up to 50% of Max Power
* Split among up to 10 curators
* Delegators share in the upside and downside of their delegatees' curation
  * Rewards
    * 10% burn fee on curator rewards earned from delegated power
    * 50/50 split on post-fee rewards earned from delegated power to curator and delegators
  * Slashing
    * Delegators are slashed based on the % that their delegatees' are slashed and the portion of the delegatee's delegated power that the delegator is contributing to them
