# ▶️ Get Started

## Onboarding

Login with Twitter/X. Enjoy a self-custody Solana wallet, secured by [Privy](https://www.privy.io/). Transactions use BANG. Fees are covered by Bangit.

To ensure authenticity, Twitter/X accounts must meet some initial requirements:

* \>90 days old
* \>100 followers

Find an invite code, or DM @bangitdotxyz

***

## Gameplay

#### How to Play

* Upvote under-upvoted bangers, Downvote over-upvoted slop
* Earn rewards for your taste
* Stake BANG for more rewards, clout, and influence on feeds

***

## Interface

#### :house: Feeds

* **Hot**: highest impact + recently upvoted + recently posted
* **Top**: highest impact
* **Bump**: recently voted
* **New**: recently posted

#### :mag\_right: Search

Find any tweet or user by link, ID, or username

Or discover tweets with a personalized AI curator
