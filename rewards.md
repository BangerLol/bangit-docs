#### Rewards are distributed daily to top curators, creators, and inviters.

Everyday at midnight UTC, the Rewards Pool is distributed to curators, creators, and inviters based on performance in the last 24 hours

Users can claim rewards at anytime. "Claim + Stake" is free, while "Claim" incurs a 10% fee on the claimable tokens. A 10% fee on unclaimed rewards is applied every 7 days they are left unclaimed.

## Curators: 80%

* Split among voters with positive Motion in the period, proportionally
* Voting is **gamified trading**. Each vote is scored based on the tweet's performance in the 24 hours after the vote.
  * **Vision** = same-direction, log-scaled, time-weighted average Net Impact change of tweet in the 24 hours after vote ‚âà **% price change of asset since trade**
  * **Taste** = Vibe \* Conviction (% of Max Power used for vote) ‚âà **% profit**
  * **Motion** = Taste \* sqrt(Power) ‚âà **$ profit**

## Creators: 10%

* Split among authors of tweets with positive net impact in the period, proportionally, scaled by Reward Boost

## Inviters: 10%

* Split among inviters of voters that earned curator rewards in the period, proportionally, scaled by invite degrees and Reward Boost
